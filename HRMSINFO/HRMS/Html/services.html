HR SERVICES

Employee Database Management

HRMS allows HR managers to store and manage employee data, including personal details, employment history, performance, and training records.

Recruitment Management

HRMS can automate the recruitment process, including job postings, resume screening, and interview scheduling.

Onboarding and Offboarding

HRMS can manage the onboarding and offboarding processes, including new employee orientation, documentation, and exit interviews.

Time and Attendance Management

HRMS can automate time tracking and attendance management, including tracking of leave requests, overtime, and absence.

Self Services

HRMS provides employees with self-service portals to view their personal information, request leave, access pay stubs and tax forms, and update their details.

Benefits Administration

HRMS can handle employee benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and other perks and ensure compliance with regulations.
